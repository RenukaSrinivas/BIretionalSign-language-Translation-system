## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Sentences

### Facial Expressions Matter:
- **Questions**: Raise eyebrows, tilt head forward
- **Statements**: Neutral face, clear gaze
- **Emotions**: Match expression to feeling
- **Urgency**: Intensify expression

### Signing Flow:
- **Pause**: Between words (like spaces)
- **Smooth**: Transition between letters
- **Clear**: Each sign distinct
- **Natural**: Don't rush

### Body Language:
- **Orientation**: Face person directly
- **Distance**: Arms length away
- **Posture**: Upright, confident
- **Space**: Use signing space (chest to head)
