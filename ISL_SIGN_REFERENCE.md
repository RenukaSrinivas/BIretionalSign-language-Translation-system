## üî§ ISL ALPHABET (A-Z)

### A
**Hand Shape:** Closed fist
**Position:** Thumb rests against side of fist
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Like holding something in fist with thumb on outside

### B
**Hand Shape:** Flat hand, all fingers together and straight
**Position:** Fingers pointing up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Like a karate chop hand, rigid and flat

### C
**Hand Shape:** Curved hand forming C shape
**Position:** Fingers curved like holding a cup
**Palm:** Facing to the side
**Visual:** Hand forms clear letter C shape

### D
**Hand Shape:** Index finger pointing up
**Position:** Thumb touches middle, ring, and pinky fingers
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** One finger up, circle formed below

### E
**Hand Shape:** Fingers curled inward
**Position:** Thumb crosses over bent fingers
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** All fingers bent, thumb on top

### F
**Hand Shape:** OK sign with index and thumb touching
**Position:** Other three fingers extended upward
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Classic "OK" gesture

### G
**Hand Shape:** Index finger and thumb extended parallel
**Position:** Pointing to the side horizontally
**Palm:** Facing inward
**Visual:** Like pinching air sideways

### H
**Hand Shape:** Index and middle fingers together
**Position:** Extended horizontally to the side
**Palm:** Facing down
**Visual:** Two fingers making a horizontal line

### I
**Hand Shape:** Fist with pinky finger extended up
**Position:** Pinky straight up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** "I love you" partial sign (just pinky)

### J
**Hand Shape:** Pinky finger extended
**Position:** Draw letter J in air with pinky
**Palm:** Facing outward
**Visual:** Write a J with pinky finger

### K
**Hand Shape:** Index and middle fingers pointing up
**Position:** Thumb extends between the two fingers
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Peace sign with thumb poking through

### L
**Hand Shape:** Index finger up, thumb out to side
**Position:** Forms letter L shape
**Palm:** Facing forward/side
**Visual:** Clear L shape with hand

### M
**Hand Shape:** Three fingers bent over thumb
**Position:** Fingers drape over thumb
**Palm:** Facing down
**Visual:** Thumb tucked under 3 fingers

### N
**Hand Shape:** Two fingers bent over thumb
**Position:** Index and middle over thumb
**Palm:** Facing down
**Visual:** Like M but only 2 fingers

### O
**Hand Shape:** All fingertips touch thumb tip
**Position:** Forms circle/O shape
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Perfect circle with hand

### P
**Hand Shape:** Like K but angled down
**Position:** Index and middle down with thumb between
**Palm:** Facing down
**Visual:** K rotated downward

### Q
**Hand Shape:** Like G but pointing down
**Position:** Index and thumb pointing down
**Palm:** Facing down
**Visual:** G rotated to point at ground

### R
**Hand Shape:** Index and middle fingers crossed
**Position:** Crossed at tips
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** First two fingers make X shape

### S
**Hand Shape:** Closed fist
**Position:** Thumb across front of fingers
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Thumb wraps over fingers

### T
**Hand Shape:** Thumb between index and middle
**Position:** Thumb pokes out between fingers
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Thumb sandwiched between first two fingers

### U
**Hand Shape:** Index and middle fingers together
**Position:** Extended upward, touching
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Two fingers together like peace sign but touching

### V
**Hand Shape:** Index and middle fingers apart
**Position:** Extended upward in V shape
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Victory/peace sign

### W
**Hand Shape:** Index, middle, and ring fingers up
**Position:** All three extended and spread
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Three fingers making W shape

### X
**Hand Shape:** Index finger bent into hook
**Position:** Crooked/bent at middle joint
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Hooked index finger

### Y
**Hand Shape:** Thumb and pinky extended
**Position:** Other fingers closed, thumb and pinky out
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** "Call me" gesture or "hang loose" sign

### Z
**Hand Shape:** Index finger extended
**Position:** Draw letter Z in air with index finger
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Write Z shape with pointing finger

## üî¢ ISL NUMBERS (0-9)

### 0
**Hand Shape:** Closed fist, all fingers in
**Position:** Fist closed tight
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Zero/nothing gesture

### 1
**Hand Shape:** Index finger extended up
**Position:** Only index finger up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Number one gesture, single finger

### 2
**Hand Shape:** Index and middle fingers up
**Position:** Two fingers extended
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Peace sign / number two

### 3
**Hand Shape:** Thumb, index, and middle extended
**Position:** Three fingers up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Three fingers showing

### 4
**Hand Shape:** All four fingers up (not thumb)
**Position:** Fingers together pointing up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Four fingers extended

### 5
**Hand Shape:** All five fingers spread
**Position:** Fingers and thumb all extended
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Open hand, all fingers out

### 6
**Hand Shape:** Thumb touches pinky
**Position:** Other three fingers extended
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Three fingers up, thumb and pinky touching

### 7
**Hand Shape:** Thumb touches ring finger
**Position:** Three fingers up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Index, middle, pinky up

### 8
**Hand Shape:** Thumb touches middle finger
**Position:** Fingers arranged
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Some fingers up, some down

### 9
**Hand Shape:** Thumb touches index finger
**Position:** Other fingers up
**Palm:** Facing forward
**Visual:** Circle with thumb and index

## üì∏ PHOTO REFERENCE GUIDE

### How to Photograph Each Sign:

**Setup:**
1. Plain white wall background
2. Bright, even lighting from front
3. Camera at chest level
4. Hand centered in frame

**Position:**
1. Make the ISL sign correctly (reference above)
2. Hold steady
3. Ensure all fingers visible
4. No shadows on hand

**Capture:**
1. Take photo
2. Review for clarity
3. Ensure sharp focus
4. Check hand position matches description

**Save:**
1. Crop to square (500x500px minimum)
2. Save as PNG with transparent background
3. Or JPG with white background
4. Name EXACTLY as letter (A.png, B.png, etc.)
